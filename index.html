ABOUT US

The Canadian-Greenlandic Empire is a powerful northern nation on CCNet, founded on May 13, 2025, through the union of Canada and Greenland. As one of the largest nations by land and population, we are known for our strategic leadership, rich culture, and ambitious development.

With Nova Valyria as our industrial capital and a government led by a federal triumvirate, we balance strength with stability. Our diverse and friendly community spans the icy expanses of Canada, Greenland, and beyond — united in building a legacy of greatness.

A HOME FOR BUILDERS
